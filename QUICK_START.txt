POSITION SIZING CHEAT SHEET
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Risk = (Entry - SL) / 0.0001 × 0.10 = Dollar Risk

Example EURUSD:
  Entry: 1.1600
  SL: 1.1550 (50 pips)
  Micro-lots needed = $3 / (50 × 0.10) = $3 / $5 = 0.6 micro-lots

Example GBPUSD:
  Entry: 1.3400
  SL: 1.3340 (60 pips)
  Micro-lots needed = $3 / (60 × 0.10) = $3 / $6 = 0.5 micro-lots

Example USDJPY:

  SL: 151.20 (30 pips)
  Micro-lots needed = $3 / (30 × 0.10) = $3 / $3 = 1.0 micro-lots
