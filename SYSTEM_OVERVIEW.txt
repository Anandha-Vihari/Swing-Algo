⚙️ POSITION SIZING
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Formula: micro_lots = risk_amount / (risk_pips × 0.10)

By Account Size:
  $20:  Risk $3/trade → 0.3 micro-lots
  $50:  Risk $5/trade → 0.5 micro-lots
  $100: Risk $10/trade → 1.0 micro-lots
  $200: Risk $20/trade → 2.0 micro-lots

Scaling Rules:
  +$10 profit: Increase to 0.4 micro-lots
  +$30 profit: Increase to 0.5 micro-lots
  +$50 profit: Increase to 0.75 micro-lots
  -$5 loss: Cut to 0.2 micro-lots
  -$10 loss: Take break (daily stop)


✅ ENTRY RULES (MECHANICAL - NO INTERPRETATION)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

LONG Entry Criteria:
  1. Identify swing low in last 15 candles
  2. Identify swing high ABOVE it
  3. Wait for close above swing high + 5 pips
  4. Confirm uptrend (2+ HH/HL patterns)
  5. Enter next candle
  6. SL: 20-30 pips below swing low
  7. TP: 1.5× risk amount


SHORT Entry Criteria:
  1. Identify swing high in last 15 candles
  2. Identify swing low BELOW it
  3. Wait for close below swing low - 5 pips
  4. Confirm downtrend (2+ LH/LL patterns)
  5. Enter next candle
  6. SL: 20-30 pips above swing high
  7. TP: 1.5× risk amount


❌ EXIT RULES (AUTOMATIC)
═════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

TP Hit:    Exit immediately (lock profit)
SL Hit:    Exit immediately (cut loss)
Reversal:  Exit if close back within swing (breakout fails)

DO NOT:
  • Move stop loss away from original level
  • Hold hoping trade reverses
  • Add to losing positions
  • Skip stop loss "just this once"
  • Scale out of winners (simple 1.5:1 exits)
